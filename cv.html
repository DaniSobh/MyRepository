Types of Resume:

Chronological CV Templates

A chronological resume lists your work experiences and achievements starting from the current or most recent one, and following up with previous jobs below.

Combination Resume Templates

A combination resume is a type of resume format that combines elements from both the chronological and functional resume formats. It highlights an individual's skills, qualifications, and accomplishments.
